Hello, my name is ProgrammerMarcus and I am a human. I like to take long walks around the beach because I enjoy the sound of the waves and the breeze. Sometimes, I do programming on my computer. While programming, I once lost my internet connection.

My favorite thing to do at the beach is to build sandcastles. I like to use buckets, shovels, and molds to create different shapes and structures. I also like to decorate them with shells, stones, seaweed, and other things I find on the beach. When I am not building sandcastles, I like to relax in the shade. Relaxing in the shade can be comfortable, especially when its hot outside.

While building a sandcastle I was once attacked by crabs. Luckily, a had already built sand catapults so I was able to defend myself in the sandcastle I was relaxing at. Unfortunately, the sandcastle collapsed and I got sand in my mouth, which humans sometimes do. This event greatly traumatized me and cause me to develop a hatred for sand. Since then, I have not visited the beach.
