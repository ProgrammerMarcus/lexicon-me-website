Hello, my name is ProgrammerMarcus and I am a human. I like to take long walks around the beach because I enjoy the sound of the waves and the breeze.

My favorite thing to do at the beach is to build sandcastles. I like to use buckets, shovels, and molds to create different shapes and structures. I also like to decorate them with shells, stones, seaweed, and other things I find on the beach.

Once while building a sandcastle I was attacked by crabs. Luckily, a had built sand catapults so I was able to defend myself.
